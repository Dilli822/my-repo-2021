Pokhara (Nepali: पोखरा, Nepali pronunciation: [ˈpokʰʌɾa]) is a metropolitan city in Nepal,
which serves as the capital of Gandaki Province. It is the country's largest metropolitan city in terms of area and second largest in terms of populations. The city also serves as the headquarters
of Kaski District.Pokhara is located 200 kilometres (120 miles) west of the capital, Kathmandu. The altitude varies from 827 metres (2,713 feet) in the southern part to 1,740 metres (5,710 feet) in the north.The Annapurna Range, with three out of the ten highest peaks in the world — Dhaulagiri,Annapurna I and Manaslu — is within 15–35 mi (24–56 km) of the valley.
